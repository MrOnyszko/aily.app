Aily

Aily is a personal journal and diary. It enables you to keep a private journal effortlessly. Pick your mood and add activities you have been doing during the day. You can also review all entries in the statistics on charts.

Track your mood on a daily basis

Aily enables you to keep track of your mood with ease. You can download this application on Google Play for free.
